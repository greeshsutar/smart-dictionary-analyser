About Our Project

AI Text Tools is an advanced platform designed to make communication easier, smarter, and more accessible. It brings together Artificial Intelligence features like translation, summarization, and text-to-speech, creating a powerful tool for students, teachers, researchers, and everyday users.

Our goal is simple: to break language barriers, help users understand content faster, and make high-level AI technology available for everyone in a clean and easy interface.

Project Features
  • Smart multi-language translation
  • AI-powered text summarization
  • Text-to-Speech audio output
  • Clean and responsive UI
  • Fast and accurate results
Our Vision

To enhance learning, communication, and productivity using intelligent AI tools that save time and bring clarity to information.
